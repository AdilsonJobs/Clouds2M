How to Activate a Client on Cloud2

Made by Clouds2 with Scribe

This inetrnal guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to activate a client on Cloud2. By following these instructions, users will be able to easily navigate the Cloud2 platform, access their client account, and set limits for their account. This guide is useful for anyone who wants to efficiently manage their client account on Cloud2.



1. Navigate to https://lad-z-mnode.clouds2africa.com/

2. Click "Menu"

3. Click "Accounts"

4. Click "+".

5. Click "Ok"

6. Account has created

7. Click "DEMO_CLDASDVJDVJSLVDNOVDSNV1"

8. Click "Create Project".

9. Fill the text field.

10. Click "Ok"

11. Click "Limits"

12. Click "Add"

13. Click "Number of cores"

14. Click "Ok"

15. Click "Add"

16. Click "Number of images".

17. Click "Ok"

18. Click "Add"

19. Click "RAM"

20. Click "Ok"

21. Click "Add"

22. Click "Number of snapshots"

23. Click this field.

24. Click "Ok"

25. Click "Add"

26. Click "Volume capacity"

27. Click this field.

28. Click "ao_angcb_lad_01_EBS_HDD_01"

29. Click "Ok"

30. Click here.

31. Click "Volume capacity"

32. Click "ao_angcb_lad_01_EBS_SSD_01"

33. Double-click this field.

34. Click "Ok"

35. Limit resume

36. Click "Menu"

37. Click "Accounts"

38. Click "DEMO_CLDASDVJDVJSLVDNOVDSNV1"

39. Click "Limits"

40. Click "Edit"

41. Click the "Unlimited" field.

42. Click "Ok"

Tip: Account is now limits set
