How to create protect resource

Made by Clouds2 with Scribe

This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to create and protect a resource. Following these steps will ensure that your resource is successfully protected and easily accessible.



1. Navigate to https://lad-z-mnode.clouds2africa.com/

2. Click "Menu"

3. Click "Groups"

4. Click "+".

5. Click "Next"

6. Define when snapshot start

7. Click "Finish"

8. Click "Menu"

9. Click "Instances"

10. Click on instance name "demo #"

11. Click More.

12. Click "Protect"

13. Select the protection Group created later

14. Click "Ok"

15. Protection updated for instance demo #

16. Click "Menu"

17. Click "Groups"

18. Click "PG-Demo"

19. Click "Protected Resources"

20. Now, demo instance appear
