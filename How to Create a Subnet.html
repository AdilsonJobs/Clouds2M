How to Create a Subnet

Made by Clouds2 with Scribe

This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to create a subnet. It includes clear and concise directions, making it easy for someone to follow along and successfully create a subnet.



1. Navigate to https://lad-z-mnode.clouds2africa.com/

2. Click "Menu"

3. Click "VPCs"

4. Select the VPC

5. Click "Modify"

6. Click "+".

7. Select the VPC

8. Insert the CIDR and Click "Ok"

9. VPC Subnet Other-Subnet has been created

10. Click "Ok"

11. Click "Subnets"

12. Subnet resume
